# Make Payments

Tempo is optimized for stablecoin payments with sub-second finality and minimal fees.

## Features

- **Instant finality** - Transactions confirm in under 1 second
- **Low fees** - Pay fractions of a cent per transaction
- **Stablecoin fees** - Pay transaction fees in stablecoins, not ETH
- **Parallel execution** - Send multiple payments simultaneously
